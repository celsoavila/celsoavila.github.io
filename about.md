# About Me

I am a Senior Financial Data Engineer and Quant Developer with extensive experience
building high-reliability systems for financial markets.

My work spans:

- **Systematic equities and factor models**  
- **Credit modeling** and receivables (including card payments)  
- **Market data engineering** and research pipelines  
- **DLT-based financial infrastructures**  
- **Portfolio construction engines** and risk analytics  

I specialize in designing production-grade architectures for:

- Data ingestion and feature engineering  
- Model training, validation and scoring  
- Backtesting and performance analysis  
- Daily or intraday portfolio generation and monitoring  

I have worked across:

- Equities  
- Credit receivables  
- Funds and multimarket strategies  
- Payments and acquiring  
- Tokenization / DLT applied to regulated financial assets  

My focus is always the same:  
**Clean, reproducible, automated and robust systems**, aligned with real-world
constraints in regulated financial environments.
